Shepherd's Pie

Description

Meat sauce covered with mashed potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion
  • 1lb ground beef
  • one tin of tomatoes
  • potatoes
  • salt & pepper

Steps

  1. make the meat sauce
  2. boil potatoes
  3. mash potatoes
  4. put meat sauce in casserolle
  5. cover with mashed potatoes
  6. bung it in the oven
