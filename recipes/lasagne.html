Lasagne

Description

Layers of pasta, bolognaise and white sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion
  • 1lb ground beef
  • one tin of tomatoes
  • several sheets of Lasagne
  • flour
  • milk
  • oil
  • italian herbs & seasoning

Steps

  1. make the meat sauce
  2. make the white sauce
  3. make a layer of each, then a layer of lasagne sheets
  4. bung it in the oven
